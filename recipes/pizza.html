Pizza

Description

Delicious pizza with nice cheese and sauce to always satisfy the demands of hunger.

Ingredients

  • 1 16 ounce ball of pizza dough
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 3 ounces thinly sliced mozzarella cheese
  • 5 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2.5 ounces pepperoni
  • 2 tablespoons shredded parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Place a pizza stone in the bottom third of your oven.
  2. Preheat your oven to 550 degrees F, or as high as it will go (at least 475.) Let the stone preheat for 30 minutes.
  3. Roll out the dough.
  4. Transfer the dough to a square of parchment paper.
  5. Top your pizza. Leave the crust on the peel while you add your toppings.
  6. Bake the pizza.
  7. Remove your pizza from the oven using the pizza peel.
